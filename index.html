Kaskada — Founder, CTO

Jan 2018 – Jan 2023 · acquired by DataStax
  • Founded Kaskada to simplify feature engineering from event-based data. Iteration with users drove the product from a managed service with a custom DSL to an open-source Python library over Rust and Apache Arrow, anchored by Timestreams — a columnar abstraction over temporally-ordered events that unified historical training-data generation with real-time feature serving, eliminated data leakage by construction, and allowed feature evaluation at arbitrary, data-dependent points in time.
  • Contributed significantly to the search for product-market fit — ongoing customer conversations, iterating on product packaging and go-to-market strategy, and aligning the engine's capabilities with what customers would actually pay for.
  • Raised two rounds of funding; scaled the team to ~20 people at peak. Led engineering through acquisition by DataStax in 2023.
